About Our Natural Juicery

Hello, we’re Natural Juicery. We’re new and fresh, just like every single one of our cold pressed juices. The Natural Juicery was founded with a spirited heart and an ambitious goal - to offer the purest, cold pressed juices. We hope to show you how incorporating our juices as a part of your daily routine will improve all aspects of your life. We believe healthiness is the root of happiness. We believe in equal opportunity Organics for all, so all may live a long beautiful life We offer 100% raw organic juices that not only do good for your health but taste great too! WHAT IS COLD PRESSED?

At Natural Juicery we use the cold-pressed method for juicing. This is the healthiest and best way to make juice from fruit and vegetables. Instead of using a centrifugal machine with high speed rotating blades, fresh fruits and vegetables are pressed using a hydraulic press juicer that applies enormous pressure to extract the nectar directly from the pulp. Therefor the fruits and vegetables are not exposed to heat, meaning the oxidation process is slowed down, leaving you with the purest and most nutritious juice possible. The cold-pressed method allows enzymes to stay alive, resulting in raw “living” juice with maximum nutrients up to 72 hours.
